Jacob Batista (born July 15th, 2002) is a Cybersecurity Student. He attends Penn State Berks, and is currently pursuing a Bachelors Degree.

At Penn State, he has studied web design, security risk analysis, computer forensics, and computer programming.

In 2021, he graduated from Wilson High School in West Lawn, Pennsylvania.

Early Life

Jacob was born in Reading, Pennsylvania, where he grew up spending 14 years of life until he moved to Sinking Spring, PA in 2016. Born from Dominican and Puerto Rican parents meant growing up with a mixed cultural identity. His father, Israel Batista, is a Dominican immigrant that moved to the United States and made a career in truck driving while his mother, Marilyn Batista, is a Puerto Rican born in New York that runs a small reselling business.

Career

Jacob has been working in fast food and retail stores outside of college. He has worked with Chipotle Grill since 2019 on and off. In 2023, Jacob was hired at Lowes as a cashier and later rose up to be a Sales Associate in the Paint department. He was awarded many bonuses and service stars for his excellent customer service. However, in August 2024, He was let go because of too many unexcused absences. In September 2024, he returned to Chipotle Grill at their newest store in Exeter, PA.
